Frequently Asked Questions

What is StructuredMan?

StructuredMan is a structured support platform built specifically for men navigating divorce. We offer a clear four-phase educational curriculum, practical tools and templates for every stage of the process, and a community of men moving forward — together. Think of it as the roadmap that no one gives men when their marriage ends.

Am I too early or too late to join?

Neither. StructuredMan supports men at every stage of the divorce journey — whether you're just considering separation, in the middle of legal proceedings, or already divorced and trying to rebuild your life. Every phase of our curriculum has something relevant for where you are right now. The earlier you start, the better your decisions tend to be — but it's never too late to get structured.

Is this a replacement for a lawyer or therapist?

No — and we'll tell you clearly when you need one. StructuredMan fills the gap between legal and therapeutic services. We give you frameworks, templates, and community support that help you ask better questions, stay organized, and make more informed decisions. We complement your attorney and therapist — we don't replace them. In fact, many men report that StructuredMan made their work with professionals far more effective and less expensive.

What makes this different from just searching online?

Search results give you fragments — disconnected articles, Reddit threads, and legal FAQ pages that don't know your situation. StructuredMan gives you a sequence: a clear, ordered curriculum built around the actual challenges men face, delivered in a structured order that mirrors the real-world journey. Plus templates you can immediately use, and a community of men who've been where you are.

Is there a community component?

Yes — and community is one of our most important features. Isolation is one of the most damaging aspects of divorce for men. StructuredMan gives you access to a community of men going through the same process — a space to ask questions, share progress, find accountability, and get real answers from people who understand what you're dealing with, without judgment.

What if I'm dealing with a high-conflict divorce?

We have specific modules and resources designed for high-conflict situations — including parental alienation, hostile co-parenting dynamics, and adversarial legal proceedings. These scenarios are more common than people think, and you won't be left to navigate them alone. Our legal partner Marcus Reed built much of this content from his direct experience representing men in exactly these situations.

Does StructuredMan help with co-parenting specifically?

Absolutely. Co-parenting is one of the most challenging long-term dimensions of divorce — and one of the most important to get right for your kids. We have a dedicated Fatherhood module that covers parenting plans, age-appropriate communication with children, co-parenting communication frameworks, and how to stay present and engaged when you're parenting across two homes.

How long does the curriculum take to complete?

StructuredMan is designed to be self-paced. The four phases are structured sequentially, but you move through them at the pace your situation demands. Some men focus intensively during the active divorce period and return to later phases as their situation evolves. There's no deadline — your access is ongoing, and different modules become relevant at different points in the journey.

What kind of tools and templates are included?

Every module comes with practical tools — not just information. These include parenting plan templates, financial tracking worksheets, asset division calculators, co-parenting communication scripts, decision checklists for major legal and financial choices, post-divorce budgeting templates, and more. Everything is designed to be immediately usable, not just informative.

How do I get started?

Fill out the form below — it takes about two minutes. We'll get you set up with access to Phase 1 of the curriculum and the community. From there, you move at your own pace. If you want to talk to someone first, use the contact section below and we'll get back to you within one business day.
