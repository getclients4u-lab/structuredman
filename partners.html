Founding Partners

Built by Experts Who've Seen It Firsthand

Each founding partner brings a distinct area of expertise — and a shared conviction that the current system leaves men without the support they need.

MR
Marcus Reed
Co-Founder & Legal Strategist

Former family law attorney with 18 years of experience representing men in high-stakes divorce proceedings across multiple jurisdictions. Marcus has seen firsthand how unprepared most men are for the legal dimensions of divorce — and built StructuredMan's entire legal curriculum to close that gap.

Before co-founding StructuredMan, Marcus led a boutique family law practice and authored widely-read guides on asset protection for men going through separation. He brings both courtroom experience and a translator's ability to make complex legal concepts immediately usable.

Areas of Expertise

Family Law Asset Protection Mediation Custody Strategy High-Conflict Cases
18 years in family law LEGAL LEAD
DK
David Kim
Co-Founder & Financial Architect

Certified Financial Planner (CFP) and Certified Divorce Financial Analyst (CDFA) with a specialty in helping men rebuild wealth after separation. David has guided hundreds of men through post-divorce financial reconstruction — from dividing complex asset portfolios to rebuilding credit and retirement accounts from scratch.

David's approach is built on one core belief: financial decisions made during divorce have consequences that last decades. His curriculum gives men the knowledge to make those decisions well — not react to them in panic.

Areas of Expertise

Financial Planning Wealth Rebuilding CDFA Asset Division Retirement Planning
CFP · CDFA certified FINANCE LEAD
JV
Jerome Vance
Co-Founder & Wellness Lead

Licensed psychologist and men's wellness coach with two decades of experience helping men process identity loss, grief, and major life transitions. Jerome has worked with hundreds of divorced men in clinical and coaching settings — and knows exactly where the standard therapeutic model falls short for men.

Jerome built StructuredMan's emotional resilience program from the ground up — making it practical, direct, and built around how men actually think and heal, not how therapists wish they would. The result is a program men actually complete.

Areas of Expertise

Psychology Men's Wellness Resilience Coaching Grief & Loss Identity Transition
20 years in men's psychology WELLNESS LEAD
