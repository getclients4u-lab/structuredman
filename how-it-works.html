Each Phase, In Depth

What You'll Do — and Why It Matters

Every phase is built around real pain points men experience during and after divorce. Here's exactly what happens at each stage.

1
Phase One

Build Awareness & Find Your Footing

Most men enter divorce with a fragmented understanding of what's actually happening — legally, financially, and emotionally. This phase is about establishing a clear, honest picture of where you stand so every decision you make is grounded in reality, not fear.

We help you identify your pain points, clarify what you actually need, map your customer journey as a divorced man, and build the foundation for everything that follows.

What This Phase Covers

  • Self-assessment tools to map your legal, financial, and emotional situation
  • Identifying your specific pain points and buying triggers
  • Building your personal "needs map" for the divorce journey
  • Understanding your options before making any major decisions
  • Setting up your initial action plan and timeline
🎯

Clarity From Day One

The most common mistake men make is acting before they have a clear picture of their situation. Phase 1 slows you down just enough to get oriented — so Phase 2 can move fast.

Expected Outcome

Higher awareness, a validated personal action plan, and the confidence to make informed decisions — not reactive ones.

Self-Assessment Needs Mapping Action Planning
2
Phase Two

Optimize the Divorce Journey

With a clear picture of your situation, it's time to build your strategy. This phase covers the end-to-end divorce process — from legal proceedings to parenting plans to financial negotiations — so you can move through it with intention rather than reaction.

We review your full journey through the process, help you optimize every decision point, and give you the frameworks to protect what matters most.

What This Phase Covers

  • Reviewing your full divorce journey and key decision points
  • Custody and parenting plan frameworks
  • Asset protection and negotiation strategy
  • Understanding when to negotiate vs. when to litigate
  • Communication frameworks for dealing with attorneys and mediators
🗺️

Strategy Over Reaction

The divorce process has leverage points most men don't know exist. This phase gives you a map of those points — and a strategy to use them in your favor.

Expected Outcome

Increased confidence in the legal and financial proceedings, better decisions, and improved outcomes for you and your children.

Legal Strategy Financial Negotiation Parenting Plans
3
Phase Three

Strengthen Your Foundation

Once the immediate legal and financial decisions are behind you, the real work begins: building a new life. This phase is about creating the systems, habits, and infrastructure that hold your new reality together — financially, physically, and emotionally.

We give you the operational scaffolding for your post-divorce life — housing, budgeting, co-parenting logistics, personal health, and the data and metrics to track your own recovery.

What This Phase Covers

  • Post-divorce financial rebuild and budgeting
  • Housing, logistics, and practical life restructuring
  • Co-parenting systems and communication tools
  • Health, fitness, and daily routine rebuilding
  • Measuring your own progress with clear milestones
🏗️

Systems That Hold

A new life doesn't build itself. This phase is about engineering the structure — day by day, system by system — so that stability becomes the baseline, not the exception.

Expected Outcome

A scalable, data-driven personal operating platform — routines, finances, and relationships you can sustain and grow.

Lifestyle Design Financial Reset Co-Parenting Systems
4
Phase Four

Create Your Growth Narrative

The final phase is about more than surviving divorce — it's about building the story of who you're becoming. This is where we help you define your identity, build momentum in your personal and professional life, and step into the future with clarity and purpose.

We help you develop your forward-looking narrative — the goals, identity, and vision that will guide your next chapter — so that divorce becomes the turning point, not the endpoint.

What This Phase Covers

  • Personal narrative and identity reframing
  • Goal setting for the next 12, 24, and 60 months
  • Career and financial growth planning
  • Relationship readiness and dating after divorce
  • Long-term vision and the man you're choosing to become
🚀

The Next Chapter Starts Here

Divorce is a transition, not a destination. Phase 4 is where men stop asking "how do I get through this?" and start asking "who do I want to be?" — and get a clear answer.

Expected Outcome

A compelling personal growth narrative and a clear path forward — with the mindset, momentum, and tools to actually get there.

Identity Rebuilding Goal Setting Forward Momentum
